33 // A G4VParticleChange dedicated to occurrence biasing : it
34 // applies weights for non-interaction over a step, and for
35 // interaction at the end of the step (if interaction occurs) on
36 // top of a given particle change, that is the one produced by a
37 // physics process (biased in its final state or not).
